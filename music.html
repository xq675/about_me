Music

Music I Play/Sing

At age 5, I discovered my love for music. Over the years, I've learned to sing musical theater and classical repertoires and to play the piano. Last year, I performed choral repertoires as the lead Soprano in NYU's World Voices Collective. You can view my recordings on my YouTube channel.

Music I Listen To

The music that I listen day-to-day is quite different from the music that I play. I like all kinds of music—music in any language, like Chinese, Korean, Japanese, and English, and music from any time period, from 2020s to 1980s.
